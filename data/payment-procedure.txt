Payment Procedure - K.R. Mangalam University

K.R. Mangalam University offers a variety of secure and convenient payment methods for all fees, including registration, tuition, hostel, transport, and other charges. These options are designed to streamline the payment process, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience for students and their families.

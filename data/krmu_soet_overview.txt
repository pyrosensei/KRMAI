School of Engineering and Technology (SOET) — KR Mangalam University
Overview and Programs

1. About SOET
   The School of Engineering and Technology (SOET) at KR Mangalam University, Gurugram, offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes in engineering and technology. The school is headed by Dean Dr. Pankaj Agarwal (Professor, Ph.D. Computer Science).

2. B.Tech Programs Offered
   a) B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) — Standard
   b) B.Tech CSE with Specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML)
   c) B.Tech CSE with Specialization in Full Stack Development
   d) B.Tech CSE with Specialization in UX/UI Design
   e) B.Tech CSE with Specialization in Cyber Security
   f) B.Tech CSE with Specialization in Data Science
   g) B.Tech CSE with Specialization in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence
   h) B.Tech CSE with Specialization in Cloud Computing
   i) B.Tech CSE with Specialization in Semiconductor Design
   - Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
   - Lateral entry available for diploma holders

3. Postgraduate Programs
   a) M.Tech in Computer Science
   b) MCA (Master of Computer Applications) — Standard
   c) MCA with Specialization

4. Doctoral Programs
   - Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering
   - Ph.D. in related engineering disciplines

10. Admission Process for B.Tech
    Step 1: Submit online application at admissions.krmangalam.edu.in
    Step 2: Pay application fee (Rs. 1,000 non-refundable)
    Step 3: Appear for KREE (KR Mangalam University Entrance Exam) if applicable
    Step 4: Attend faculty interview
    Step 5: Receive offer letter
    Step 6: Complete enrollment and pay registration fee

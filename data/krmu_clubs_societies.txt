Clubs and Societies — KR Mangalam University

KR Mangalam University offers 14 student clubs designed to develop leadership, teamwork, communication, organizational, and time management skills. Students can join clubs during the registration period at the start of each semester (for freshers: 11th-13th September).

1. MUN (Model United Nations) Club
   - Focus: Debates, discussions, and critical thinking
   - Activities: Extempore competitions, panel discussions, quizzes, poster-making
   - Convener: Ms. Aarti Lamba (SOLS)
   - Co-convener: Mr. Anurag Sharma
   - Email: mun@krmangalam.edu.in

2. DIA Club (Design, Interior, Architecture)
   - Focus: Design solutions to improve quality of life
   - Activities: Origami workshops, photography, film-making, sustainability competitions
   - Convener: Ms. Annu Yadav
   - Co-convener: Mr. Deepanshu Sharma

3. Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Science Society
   - Focus: Bridging theoretical learning and practical science application
   - Activities: Lectures, workshops, debates, model exhibitions, science screenings
   - Convener: Dr. Divyanshi Mangla
   - Co-convener: Dr. Saba Rashid
   - Email: apjabdulkalamsciencesociety@krmangalam.edu.in

4. Rhetoric Club
   - Focus: Communication and presentation skills development
   - Activities: Dialogue writing, group discussion, essay competitions, monologue performances
   - Convener: Dr. Anumeha Mathur
   - Co-convener: Dr. Ambika Bhatnagar
   - Email: rhetoricclub@krmangalam.edu.in

5. Health Society
   - Focus: Health awareness and physical well-being
   - Activities: Health camps (partnered with Medanta and Medox Hospital), biochemical tests
   - Co-convener: Dr. Sunil Kumar
   - Email: health.society@krmangalam.edu.in

6. Tech Nexus
   - Focus: Advancing computer science knowledge and skills
   - Activities: Coding competitions, LAN gaming, tech talks, programming contests, workshops
   - Open to all tech enthusiasts across departments

7. Mediaverse Club
   - Focus: Traditional and new media exploration
   - Activities: Photography workshops, film-making, visual storytelling, radio competitions
   - Convener: Dr. Ritika Chaudhary
   - Co-convener: Dr. Srishti Srivastava
   - Email: mediaverseclub@krmangalam.edu.in

8. Management Society
   - Focus: Managerial and entrepreneurial capabilities
   - Activities: Business quizzes, workshops, guest lectures, management events
   - Convener: Dr. Mansi Yadav
   - Co-convener: Dr. Garima
   - Email: managementsociety@krmangalam.edu.in

9. Sports Club
   - Focus: Athletic competitions and fitness activities
   - Activities: Inter-university sports events (cricket, football, basketball, badminton, chess)
   - Director: Mr. Puneet Kumar
   - Incharge: Mr. Jagjeet Singh
   - Email: sports@krmangalam.edu.in

10. Cultural Club
    - Focus: Artistic expression platform
    - Activities: Singing, dancing, theatre, drama performances
    - Co-convener: Dr. Ojasvi Dixit
    - Email: cultural.club@krmangalam.edu.in

11. Environment Club
    - Focus: Sustainable practices and environmental consciousness
    - Activities: Cleanliness drives, NGO visits, street plays, recycling initiatives, expert lectures
    - Convener: Dr. Jogender Singh Yadav
    - Co-convener: Dr. Jay Nath Patel
    - Email: environmentclub@krmangalam.edu.in

12. Chetna Society
    - Focus: Values, attitudes, and spiritual growth
    - Activities: Sessions with IAS officers, stress management programs, partnerships with ISKCON

13. Mélange Club (Fashion Club)
    - Focus: Blending artistry with sustainability in fashion
    - Activities: Fashion shows, upcycling workshops, modeling classes, trend forecasting

14. Economic Society
    - Focus: Interdisciplinary economic and policy engagement
    - Activities: Debates, data analysis workshops, research seminars, case competitions, Economics Fest

How to Join:
- Contact club conveners directly via email addresses listed above.
- Visit the club registration desk during the orientation period (for freshers: September).
- Any current student of KR Mangalam University can join one or more clubs.

Campus Facilities and Sports — KR Mangalam University

   - Centrally air-conditioned buildings
   - Established: 2013 under the Haryana Private University Act
   - 11 Schools across diverse disciplines
   - 700+ faculty members
   - 100+ multidisciplinary programs offered

   b) Smart Classrooms:
      - Ergonomic seating arrangements
      - Interactive smart boards and projectors
      - High-speed Wi-Fi connectivity in all classrooms

   c) Research Facilities:
      - 100+ specialized research labs
      - 4,000+ research publications by university faculty
      - Rs. 16 Crore+ in research and consultancy grants
      - Centres of Excellence in Robotics & Automation and Artificial Intelligence

Fee Structure — KR Mangalam University
Academic Year 2026-27

1. B.Tech Engineering Programs (Per Semester Fees)

   a) B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering (Standard)
      - Tuition Fee: Rs. 1,25,000 per semester
      - Annual Cost: Rs. 2,50,000

   b) B.Tech CSE with Specializations
      (AI & ML, Full Stack Development, UX/UI, Cyber Security, Data Science, Robotics & AI, Cloud Computing, Semiconductor Design)
      - Tuition Fee: Rs. 1,35,000 to Rs. 1,45,000 per semester
      - Annual Cost: Rs. 2,70,000 to Rs. 2,90,000

   c) B.Tech Lateral Entry
      - Same fee as direct admission for the respective track

2. Postgraduate Engineering Programs (Annual Fees)

   a) M.Tech Computer Science
      - Annual Fee: Rs. 1,20,000

   b) MCA (Standard)
      - Annual Fee: Rs. 1,30,000

   c) MCA with Specialization
      - Annual Fee: Rs. 1,65,000

3. One-Time Charges (Paid at Admission)

   - Application Fee (Non-Refundable): Rs. 1,000
   - Alumni Fee (Non-Refundable): Rs. 2,000
   - Security Deposit (Refundable at graduation): Rs. 10,000
   - Registration Fee: Rs. 25,000 (adjusted against first semester fees)

4. Hostel Fees

   - Hostel Fee (Per Annum): Rs. 1,72,000
   - Hostel Security Deposit (Refundable): Rs. 20,000

5. Transport Fees

   - Full Transport (Per Annum): Rs. 54,000
   - Shuttle Transport (Per Annum): Rs. 27,000

6. Important Notes

   - The fee payable in subsequent years may increase up to 10% per annum, subject to approval by the competent authority.
   - Payment deadline: Within 30 days of semester commencement.
   - Payment methods: Online payment via university portal (UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit Card), Demand Draft, or EMI option (max 3 installments per semester).

7. Refund Policy

   - Full refund (minus Rs. 1,000 processing fee) if withdrawal within 15 days of admission.
   - 50% refund if withdrawal within 30 days.
   - No refund after 30 days.
   - Hostel security deposit refunded at the time of leaving hostel.

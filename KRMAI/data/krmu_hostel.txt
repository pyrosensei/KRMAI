Hostel Facilities — KR Mangalam University

1. Hostel Types
   - Gayatri Hostel: For female students
   - Vivekanand Hostel: For male students
   Both hostels are conveniently located within the university campus, making it easy for students to attend classes, use campus facilities, and engage in campus activities.

2. Room Configuration
   - Each hostel room accommodates four students (quad sharing)
   - Room includes: Four single beds, individual study desks, chairs, and personal storage space
   - Twin sharing amenity facilities (attached washrooms shared between two rooms)

3. Hostel Fees
   - Annual Hostel Fee: Rs. 1,60,000
   - Hostel Security Deposit (Refundable): Rs. 20,000
   - Hostel fee is payable annually at the time of admission/renewal

4. Facilities and Amenities
   a) Internet: Free high-speed Wi-Fi provided in all hostel buildings
   b) Security:
      - Biometric access control at all entry points
      - 24/7 CCTV surveillance cameras
      - Round-the-clock security personnel
   c) Recreation and Sports:
      - Swimming Pool
      - Badminton courts
      - Table Tennis
      - Chess and Carrom
      - Foosball tables
      - Basketball court
      - Football ground
      - Cricket pitch
      - Volleyball court
      - Pickleball court
      - Lawn Tennis court
   d) Fitness:
      - On-campus gymnasium with modern equipment

5. Mess and Food
   - Mess facility available within the hostel premises
   - Meal timings and menu managed by the hostel administration
   - Special diet requests can be submitted to the mess committee

6. Hostel Rules (General)
   - Students must carry their ID cards at all times within the hostel
   - Gate timings and curfew hours are enforced by the warden
   - Night-out passes require prior approval from the warden
   - Visitors must register at the hostel reception
   - Alcohol, tobacco, and narcotics are strictly prohibited
   - Electric cooking appliances are not allowed in rooms
   - Noise restrictions apply after 10 PM

Scholarship Information — KR Mangalam University
Academic Year 2026-27

Note: Scholarships are available only for the first 100 students on a first-come-first-served basis. Scholarships apply only to first-year programme fees.

1. UNDERGRADUATE SCHOLARSHIPS

   Part A: Merit-Based Scholarships (Based on Class XII Marks)
   - 90% and above: 100% scholarship on tuition fees
   - 85% to 89.99%: 35% scholarship on tuition fees
   - 80% to 84.99%: 25% scholarship on tuition fees
   Payment: In 2-3 installments. First installment (25%) upon document verification, deducted from semester fee. Remaining by cheque in subsequent semesters.

   Part B: Category-Based Scholarships (Non-Merit)
   - Shero Scholarship (for female students with 70%+ in XII): 10% fee waiver
   - Siblings Scholarship (excluding first child enrolled): 15% fee waiver
   - KR Mangalam Group Alumni: 15% fee waiver
   - Defense Children: 15% fee waiver
   - Staff Children: 15% fee waiver
   - Sports Quota: 10% to 100% depending on competition level
     * National level: Higher percentage
     * International level: Up to 100%

   Part C: Entrance Exam-Based Scholarships
   - JEE Main:
     * 95.01+ percentile: 100% scholarship
     * 90.01 to 95 percentile: 50% scholarship
     * 80.01 to 90 percentile: 25% scholarship
   - CUET-1 (Best 4 Subjects):
     * 90%+ score: Up to 100% scholarship
   - CUET-2 (Percentile-Based):
     * 95+ percentile: Up to 100% scholarship
   - CLAT:
     * Rank 1 to 2,500: Up to 100% scholarship
   - NCHMCT:
     * Rank 1 to 1,000: Up to 100% scholarship
   - NATA:
     * Score 130.01+: Up to 100% scholarship

2. POSTGRADUATE SCHOLARSHIPS

   MBA (Based on Graduation Score):
   - 95%+ graduation score: 100% scholarship (3 installments)
   - 85% to 94.99%: 35% scholarship (2 installments)
   - 75% to 84.99%: 25% scholarship (2 installments)

   Non-MBA PG (Based on Graduation Score):
   - 75%+ graduation score: 25% scholarship
   - 65% to 74.99%: 10% scholarship

   PG Entrance Exam-Based:
   - CAT/MAT/XAT 95.01+ percentile: Up to 100% scholarship
   - CUET graduates 50%+: 10% to 25% scholarship

   Alumni Scholarship (KRMU UG to PG):
   - KRMU undergraduate students: 10% fee waiver for PG admissions

3. ELIGIBILITY AND CONDITIONS FOR CONTINUATION

   For 2nd and 3rd installments, students must fulfil ALL criteria:
   - Minimum 75% attendance in preceding semester
   - Cleared ALL offered subjects (no backlogs)
   - No disciplinary action or academic misconduct

4. RESTRICTIONS

   - Not applicable for D.Pharmacy or M.Pharmacy programmes
   - Part A and Part C not applicable for LLB (Hons.) or B.Ed
   - Only ONE scholarship per student — cannot combine multiple scholarships
   - Sports scholarships require certificate verification and physical testing

5. HOW TO APPLY

   Scholarships are awarded upon:
   - Document verification during admission
   - Merit evaluation based on marks/scores
   - Sports scholarships require certificate verification plus physical testing
   Contact: Scholarship Office or Admissions Portal at admissions.krmangalam.edu.in

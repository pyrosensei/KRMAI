Admissions — KR Mangalam University

1. University Overview
   KR Mangalam University was established in 2013 under the Haryana Private University Act. The university is committed to transformative education and nurturing future leaders.
   - Campus: 28 acres on Sohna Road, Gurugram, Haryana 122103
   - Total Schools: 11
   - Total Programs: 100+ multidisciplinary programmes
   - Learning Management System: lms.krmangalam.edu.in

3. Admission Process
   Step 1: Register online through the admissions portal at admissions.krmangalam.edu.in
   Step 2: Submit your academic credentials and pay the application fee (Rs. 1,000, non-refundable)
   Step 3: Appear for KREE (KR Mangalam University Entrance Exam) if applicable
   Step 4: Attend faculty interview (for applicable programmes)
   Step 5: Receive your admission offer letter
   Step 6: Complete enrollment by paying the registration fee (Rs. 25,000, adjusted in first semester fees)

4. Required Documents for Admission
   - Class X marksheet and certificate
   - Class XII marksheet and certificate
   - Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous institution
   - Migration Certificate
   - Character Certificate
   - Aadhaar Card
   - Passport-size photographs (4-6 copies)
   - Category certificate (if applicable: SC/ST/OBC/EWS)
   - Entrance exam scorecard (JEE Main/CUET/KREE if applicable)

5. Admission Fees
   - Application Fee (One-time, Non-Refundable): Rs. 1,000
   - Registration Fee: Rs. 25,000 (adjusted in semester fees upon admission)
   - Security Deposit (Refundable): Rs. 10,000
   - Alumni Fee (One-time, Non-Refundable): Rs. 2,000

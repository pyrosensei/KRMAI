PLACEMENT PROCESS
-----------------
The placement process at KRMU follows a structured four-step path:

1. Pre-Placement Talk — Companies give an overview of their organization, job roles, and expectations.
2. Written Test — Aptitude, technical, and domain-specific assessments.
3. Interview — Personal interviews (technical and HR rounds).
4. Group Discussion — Evaluation of communication, leadership, and teamwork skills.

PLACEMENT POLICY
----------------
- Career Development Center (CDC) manages all placement activities at KRMU.
- Recruitment through CDC is available only for registered KRMU students.
- Students must register with the CDC in their final year.
- The placement process commences in September every year.
- Orientation and Policy talk is organized at the beginning of every academic session.
- Students who register for a company visit MUST attend on the scheduled day; failure to attend may result in blacklisting for the next two recruitment cycles.
- The CDC provides:
  * Personal interaction with the placement team
  * Standard resume format guidance
  * Group discussion practice
  * Mock interview sessions
  * Employer visit coordination
